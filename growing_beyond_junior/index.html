Growing Beyond Junior

Sep 19, 2015

I've previously written about what a junior developer should know and what makes a great programmer. Today, I want to talk about the path from being a junior developer to being a mid-level developer.

Let’s start out by assuming that you are a junior developer without a job. First step is to find a way that you can code full time for a living. This will allow you to focus on the craft of programming all day every day.

When looking for a job, you’ll probably see a lot of listings looking for senior developers, and not as many looking for junior developers. Senior developers are very hard to find these days. If you find a company that you’d want to work with, apply to them regardless of if they are explicitly advertising a junior position.
